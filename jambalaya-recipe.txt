Ingredients:
—4 fully cooked chicken sausage links sliced into coins (Sweet Italian Chicken Sausage is best)
—One 14.5 oz can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
—3/4 cup onion, chopped
—1 large bell pepper (any color)
—1 cup celery, chopped
—1 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth
—1 cup uncooked white rice
—1 tbsp garlic, minced
—1 tsp cajun seasoning
—1/4 tsp dried oregano
—1/4 tsp dried thyme
—1/2 tsp hot sauce 
—1 1/2 cup raw shrimp, tails removed, deveined

Directions:
1. Add all ingredients except shrimp into a large pot on the stove. Mix and bring to boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium low. Cover and simmer until vegetables are tender and rice is cooked. About 35-40 minutes. 
3. Add shrimp and re-cover. Continue to cook until shrimp is tender, about 10 minutes. 
4. Season with salt, pepper, and hot sauce to taste.
5. Enjoy
